IBM PC AT: A NEW ERA IN HOME COMPUTING?

By Byte Reporter | Staff Writer

The launch of the IBM Personal Computer AT has sent ripples through the burgeoning home computing market, promising unprecedented power for the serious enthusiast and business user alike...

...Full article text about the IBM PC AT. With its cutting-edge 80286 processor and a whopping 512KB of RAM, the AT is poised to redefine what a personal computer can achieve. Early reports from industry analysts suggest this machine will be a workhorse for scientific applications and complex spreadsheets, moving beyond the simple games and word processing of its predecessors.

However, the hefty price tag may put it out of reach for the average consumer, leading some to question its mass market appeal. Only time will tell if the average American is ready for such computational might in their living room.
